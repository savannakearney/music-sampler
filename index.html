Zac Brown Band

Image courtesy of Wikimedia Commons.

Zac Brown Band is an American country band, formed in the early 2000's, and features Zac Brown as lead vocals and guitar, along with seven other bass, guitar, percussion and fiddle players. They've won three Grammy Awards and have been nominated for a total of 55 awards from Grammys, Academy of Country Music, American Music Awards, Country Music Association and Country Music Television. Zac Brown Band is the the second act ever to top both the country and active rock formats. They are currently on a 40+ date tour called "Welcome Home."
